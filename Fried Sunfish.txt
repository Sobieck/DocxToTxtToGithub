Fried Sunfish-Grandma Ronnie Sobieck

Sunfish
Cracker crumbs, eggs, oil


Catch the fish!!  Filet them and wash. Dip fish into egg, then cracker crumbs(saltines work well, crush them with rolling pin), and place in hot frying pan with oil.  Brown both sides.  Serve with tartar sauce:  Mayo, dill pickles and a little chopped onion.
